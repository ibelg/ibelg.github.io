IAT355 Assignment 3

Video Game Sales — Vega-Lite Visual Explorations

Visualization 1A: Global Sales by Genre and Platform

Prompt 1: Which platforms dominate global sales within each genre?

Prompt 2: Are some genres more dependent on specific platforms than others?

This chart shows how total video game sales are distributed across platforms within each genre. By stacking platform contributions, it reveals which systems dominate each genre and whether success is concentrated on a single platform or spread across many. The visualization highlights patterns of platform dominance and shows how genre popularity varies across gaming ecosystems.

Visualization 1B: Average Global Sales per Game by Genre

Prompt 3: Which genres generate the highest average sales per game on different platforms?

This chart compares the average sales per game for the top-performing platforms within each genre. Instead of showing total volume, it highlights where individual titles perform strongest. This makes it easier to see which platforms produce blockbuster releases and whether success comes from many games or a few high-impact hits.

Visualization 2A: Sales Over Time by Platform and Genre

Prompt 1: For a selected platform, how does the genre mix of global sales change over time?

Prompt 2: Do platforms develop distinct genre signatures during their peak years?

This visualization shows how the mix of game genres contributing to a platform’s sales changes over time. Each year represents 100% of that platform’s sales, allowing you to see how genre dominance shifts throughout its lifecycle. The chart reveals how platforms develop distinct identities and how player preferences evolve.

Visualization 2B: Platform Sales Lifespan

Prompt 3: Which platforms have the longest sales lifespan, meaning they stay strong across many years instead of briefly peaking?

This chart measures platform longevity by counting how many years each platform stays above 20% of its peak annual sales. Rather than focusing on a single peak moment, it highlights which systems remained strong over time. This helps distinguish short-lived spikes from platforms with sustained relevance.

Visualization 3A: Regional Sales vs. Platform

Prompt 1: How do platform sales distributions differ across global regions?

Prompt 2: What regional market preferences can be identified from platform sales patterns?

This visualization compares platform sales across North America, Europe, Japan, and other regions. Splitting regions into panels makes it easier to see differences in market preferences and identify which platforms dominate each region. The chart reveals how platform success varies across global markets.

Visualization 3B: Regional Platform Specialization

Prompt 3: Which platforms demonstrate regional specialization rather than global popularity?

This heatmap shows where platforms perform disproportionately well compared to their global share. Darker cells indicate platforms that are especially popular in a specific region. Instead of showing raw sales, the visualization highlights regional preferences and helps identify platforms that thrive in particular markets.

Visualization 4A: Regional Platform Specialization

Prompt 1: Which platforms are regionally specialized relative to their global baseline?

Prompt 2: What does regional specialization suggest about market segmentation across regions (NA/EU/JP/Other)?

This heatmap shows how platform popularity differs across regions by comparing each platform’s regional sales share to its global share. Values above 1 indicate that a platform is more popular in a region than expected worldwide, while lower values indicate under-representation. By focusing on proportional differences rather than raw sales, the visualization highlights regional preferences and reveals how platform success is shaped by cultural alignment and local gaming ecosystems.

Visualization 4B: Global Gaming Culture Flow

Prompt: Which regions act as dominant exporters of gaming culture versus niche markets with localized platform ecosystems?

This chart shows how global video game sales are distributed across regions. It reveals which regions represent the largest share of the global market and which reflect more localized consumption. Together with regional specialization patterns, it highlights how gaming culture and industry influence are unevenly distributed worldwide.
