IAT355 Assignment 3

Video Game Sales — Vega-Lite Visual Explorations

Visualization 1: Global Sales by Genre and Platform

Prompt 1: Which platforms dominate global sales within each genre?

Prompt 2: Are some genres more dependent on specific platforms than others?

This visualization shows how global video game sales are distributed across platforms within each genre. The stacked bars reveal which platforms contribute the most to each genre’s sales and whether success is concentrated on a single platform or spread across multiple systems. This helps highlight patterns of platform dominance and genre dependence.

Visualization 2: Sales Over Time by Platform and Genre

Prompt 1: For a selected platform, how does the genre mix of global sales change over time?

Prompt 2: Do platforms develop distinct genre signatures during their peak years?

This visualization shows how the mix of game genres contributing to a platform’s sales changes over time. Each year represents 100% of that platform’s sales, and the coloured layers show what share each genre contributes. This makes it easier to spot shifts in a platform’s identity over the years.

Visualization 3: Regional Sales vs. Platform

Prompt 1: How do platform sales distributions differ across global regions?

Prompt 2: What regional market preferences can be identified from platform sales patterns?

This visualization compares platform sales across North America, Europe, Japan, and other regions to highlight geographic differences in market preferences. Splitting regions into separate panels makes it easier to compare which platforms dominate each market.

Visualization 4: Regional Platform Specialization (Visual Story)

Prompt 1: Which platforms are regionally specialized relative to their global baseline?

Prompt 2: What does regional specialization suggest about market segmentation across regions (NA/EU/JP/Other)?

This heatmap compares each platform’s share of sales in a region to its share globally. Values above 1 mean the platform is disproportionately popular in that region, while values below 1 mean it is under represented. The goal is to highlight regional home bases and market segmentation patterns rather than just raw sales totals.
